About The Event

CONNECT – CONVERGE – CREATE: EMPOWERING THE FUTURE WITH TECHNOLOGY

VGIC 2025 is proud to bring together the TOP 100 INNOVATORS – Vietnamese and Vietnamese-origin people with global influence in the field of innovation across Semiconductors, Generative AI, and Fintech. This exclusive forum creates a platform for building influential relationships and driving transformative solutions.

Celebrating groundbreaking contributions to global and Vietnamese innovation, the VGIC 2025 Award honors individuals who are bridging the gap between Vietnam and the world—bringing global innovations closer to Vietnam while showcasing Vietnam's talent and ingenuity on the international stage.

GOALS & ASPIRATIONS

With the mission of inspiring and leading solutions to promote the application of science and technology, VGIC 2025 focuses on the central theme "Connect - Converge - Create: Empowering the Future with Technology."

Connect

to promote extensive cooperation and explore practical directions for helping Vietnam effectively participate in the global semiconductor supply chain, AI ecosystem, and fintech network.

Converge

and create a platform where new ideas and technologies intersect, creating synergy in innovative solutions based on AI, semiconductor and fintech - the main drivers of the country's sustainable development and prosperity in the new era.

Create

sustainable values ​​by leveraging innovation, transforming knowledge into action, and building practical strategies, affirming Vietnam's position on the world technology map.
